Privacy Policy

Perimeter · Last updated April 2026

The short version

Perimeter collects no personal data. Nothing leaves your Mac. There are no servers, no accounts, and no analytics.

Calendar and Reminders access

Perimeter requests access to your macOS Calendar and Reminders solely to display upcoming events in the app and trigger the glow effect. This data is read locally on your device and is never transmitted anywhere.

Purchases

In-app purchases are handled entirely by Apple through the App Store. Mighty Pickle never sees or stores your payment information.

Crash reports

If you opt in to sharing diagnostics with developers through macOS, Apple may forward anonymized crash logs to us. These contain no personally identifiable information.
