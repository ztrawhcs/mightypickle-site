TollCast Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 28, 2026

Location

TollCast uses your location while the app is in use to detect your nearest I-66 interchange. Location data is processed on-device and is never sent to our servers or shared with any third party.

In-App Purchases

Purchases are processed by Apple. We do not collect or store any payment information.

Push Notifications

If you enable notifications, your device's push token is sent to our server solely to deliver toll price alerts. It is not used for advertising or shared with third parties.

Analytics

We do not use third-party analytics or advertising SDKs. We collect no personal information.

Contact

Questions? Email support@mightypickle.dev.
