TollCast Support

TollCast is an I-66 express lane toll tracker for Northern Virginia commuters.

For help, questions, or feedback, email support@mightypickle.dev and we'll get back to you within one business day.

Common questions:

Where does the toll data come from?
Toll prices are sourced directly from VDOT's live feed and updated continuously during peak hours.

Why is the app I-66 only?
I-66 support is live now. I-495, I-95, and I-395 are coming in the next update.

How do I restore my Founder purchase?
Open the app, tap Settings, then tap Restore Purchases at the bottom of the screen.
